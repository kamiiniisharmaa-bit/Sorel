About Sorel

Heritage · Craft · Responsibility

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sorel was born from a passion for timeless design and uncompromising quality. What started as a small workshop in Amsterdam has grown into a brand loved by adventurers, city dwellers, and everyone in between. Our name, inspired by the rugged yet refined spirit of the North, reflects our commitment to creating footwear that lasts.

We believe that great shoes tell a story — of places walked, paths forged, and memories made. Every pair of Sorel shoes is crafted with intention, blending traditional techniques with modern innovation.

Our Values

  • Quality first: We use premium materials and meticulous craftsmanship.
  • Sustainable future: We are constantly reducing our environmental footprint.
  • Inclusive design: Every foot deserves comfort and style.
  • Community: We support local artisans and give back to our neighborhoods.

By 2030, we aim to use 100% sustainable or recycled materials in all our products.

Craftsmanship

Each Sorel shoe is a testament to the skill of our makers. From hand-stitched details to carefully selected leathers and eco-friendly fabrics, we oversee every step of production. Our workshops in Portugal and the Netherlands combine centuries-old techniques with cutting-edge technology to deliver footwear that performs as beautifully as it looks.
